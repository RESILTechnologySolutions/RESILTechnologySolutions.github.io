About

RESIL Technology Solutions

RESIL Technology Solutions is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Founded in 2026, we specialize in cloud infrastructure, software development, and IT consulting services for both government and commercial clients.

As a sole-owner firm actively pursuing federal subcontracting opportunities, we bring a focused, hands-on approach to every engagement. We work closely with prime contractors in the Milwaukee metro area and across Wisconsin to deliver technical capability where it's needed most.

Our team combines computer science fundamentals, active cloud platform experience, and a commitment to continuous learning — backed by an ongoing certification pathway in AWS, Terraform, and CompTIA cloud technologies.

We hold six active NAICS codes covering cloud computing, custom software, IT consulting, network systems integration, data processing, and technical training.
